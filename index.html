Why the Box Model Matters

Every element on a webpage is basically a box. Understanding how margins, borders, and padding work will save you a lot of headaches when things aren’t lining up the way you expect.

Margin

The space outside the border. Think of it as the personal bubble for your element.

Border

The actual edge of the box. You can style it, change its thickness, or make it disappear.

Padding

The space between the content and the border. Great for giving text some breathing room.
